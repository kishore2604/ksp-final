    THE INDIAN POLICE SERVICE

    Recruitment

    Appointment to the Indian Police Service shall be either by direct recruitment or by promotion from the Tamilnadu State Police Service.






    Recruitment

    Direct Recruitment to the Indian Police Service is made by the Central Government on the results of a combined competitive examination conducted by the Union Public Service Commission.




     

    TAMILNADU STATE POLICE SERVICES

    Recruitment

    Appointment to the State Police Service will be made by the State Government either by direct recruitment through the Tamilnadu Public Service Commission or by promotion in accordance with the provisions of the Tamilnadu State Police Services including Ministerial Services (Recruitment) Rules, 1994 and Tamilnadu Recruitment of Gazetted Probationers (Appointment by Competitive Examinations).

    Recruitment

    Final selection is based on the cumulative performance of candidates across all stages, including the written exam, PET, interview, and document verification, with successful candidates receiving appointment letters for the Tamilnadu Police Service.





     

    RECRUITMENT OF JUNIOR POLICE OFFICERS

    Recruitment by Promotion

    Recruitment by promotion in the Tamilnadu Police Department typically involves the advancement of existing police personnel to higher ranks or positions within the department based on their experience, performance, and eligibility criteria set forth by the departmental regulations.


    By Direct Recruitment

    The process of hiring candidates from outside the department to fill vacant positions involves conducting examinations, physical tests, interviews, and document verification procedures to assess the suitability of candidates for various roles within the department.
